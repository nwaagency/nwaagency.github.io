Access Control Systems

Modern Access Security

Access control security systems have displaced metal keys and deadbolts replacing them with key cards, electronic pass-codes, and monitoring. This allows you to select who can enter certain areas or even specify the hours that a certain space is available. Electronic locks cannot be compromised like deadbolts. Our systems provide a main dashboard for movement information.

Vehicle Tracking Solutions

GPS/GPRS SiRF Star III

We provide GPS, GPRS satellite based vehicle and asset tracking solutions. Our tracking units are fitted with the latest SIRF star III technology chips.

Features

  • Real Time Tracking: Via Online (TCP/UDP) or SMS.
  • Current location report: Get real-time location always.
  • Engine Cut: Gradual stop immobilisation via SMS.
  • Geo Fencing: Alerts when entering/leaving zones.
  • SOS Panic Button: Alert police or listen-in function.
  • Fuel & Mileage: Online monitoring and calculation.
  • Safety Alerts: Speeding, Tilt/Shaking (Accident).
  • Low Battery Alert: Notification on battery disconnect.
  • Data Logger: Stores 260,000+ waypoints if GPRS is lost.
  • Motion Sensor: For GPRS data saving.
  • Blind Area Alert: Signal sent when GPRS re-establishes.
  • Voice Broadcast: Automatic according to GPS position.

Who is this for?

  • Fleet Owners: Monitor lorries and large fleets.
  • Taxi Operators: Monitor movement and recovery.
  • Private Companies: Control movement at all times.
  • NGOs: Ensure safety of vehicles always.
  • Individuals: One stop shop for vehicle security.
